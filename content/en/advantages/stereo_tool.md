Stereo Tool is a de facto standard among audio processors for online streaming, and support for it is built into our Internet Radio Platform. This processor can significantly improve the sound quality of online radio. It is configured using preset files available online; radio owners also share presets in our Telegram channel. Many features are free, while a license key unlocks additional settings. Once you hear the improvement, returning to unprocessed audio may be difficult for both you and your listeners.
