All our users can create mobile applications for Android and iOS that are deeply integrated with our internet radio control panel. We have created our own application engine, optimized for radio listening and the requirements of mobile devices. It supports features such as automatic reconnection when the stream drops and full Bluetooth integration. Our applications are 100% native Android and iOS apps. We have been developing applications since 2011 and have already published more than 200 apps on Google Play and the Apple App Store. We offer both inexpensive, customizable apps with standard functionality (logos, streams, and branding) and fully custom applications of any complexity.
